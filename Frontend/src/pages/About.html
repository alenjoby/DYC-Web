Nela Mariam

Senior Architect | Founder

Beginning from 2009, Architect Nela Mariam has been part of a series of interesting projects including Homes, Workplaces and Hospitality Interiors.


DYC Architects, founded by Nela Mariam and based in the cultural heart of Thrissur, is a forward-thinking architectural firm dedicated to creating spaces that inspire and endure. At DYC, we believe architecture is more than just design—it's about crafting experiences that seamlessly blend functionality, aesthetics, and innovation.
Rooted in a commitment to excellence and sustainability, our team brings together a diverse range of expertise to deliver projects that are as unique as our clients' visions. From contemporary residences to commercial marvels, every project is a testament to our passion for detail and our pursuit of timeless architecture.
Driven by creativity, shaped by collaboration, and grounded in a deep respect for context and culture, DYC Architects continues to redefine the architectural landscape of Thrissur and beyond.
